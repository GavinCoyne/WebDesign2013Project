Scrap Must Be
Emptied: Drained of Oil:
  • Drums (clean)

Preparation notes
  • Rear end drained of oil, center cap removed
  • Cable, Wire & Strapping can be in coils or bundles that are securely bound and a maximum diameter of 35 inches
#1 steel Specification
  • Rear ends, hydraulic cylinders and all other sealed units must be cut to half
  • Pipe, over 8" diameter must be cut to lay flat
  • Pieces must be 1/4" thick and no larger than 5 feet by 2 feet
