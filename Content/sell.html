What we sell:

Resale yard:

Our resale yard is stocked with assorted sized pipe, plates, beams, screens, pulleys, hydraulic parts, and other used equipment.

Wholesale loads:

Kamloops Scrap Iron processes copper, brass, and aluminum in many ways. Our baling operation produces both hard and soft bales. Using our sweat furnace we manufacture aluminum ingots. Insulated copper can be processed through our wire chopping line.

Our 540 ton guillotine shear prepares scrap steel for loading into railcars on our siding. We are serviced by both national railways - Canadian National and Canadian Pacific.

Our location in central British Columbia, gives us ready access to road and rail, making overseas container shipment effective and reasonable.
